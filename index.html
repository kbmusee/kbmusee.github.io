Watercolor
TRANSPARENT
BEAUTY
Watercolor painting is admired for its delicate and translucent qualities. Artists use water to blend colors seamlessly, creating ethereal effects and soft gradients. This medium is often used to capture landscapes, botanicals, and expressive portraits.
Acrylic
VIBRANT
HUES
Acrylic paintings are known for their vibrant colors and versatility. This medium allows for a range of textures and techniques, making it a favorite among contemporary artists. It dries quickly, enabling artists to layer colors and create dynamic compositions.
